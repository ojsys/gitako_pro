history Our Story

Built by Farmers, for Farmers

Gitako was born from firsthand experience with the challenges facing African agriculture. Our founders saw the gap between traditional farming and modern technology, and set out to bridge it.

1
The Problem

African farmers lacked access to digital tools, leading to inefficient farming practices, poor record keeping, and limited market access.

2
The Vision

We envisioned a comprehensive platform that would be accessible, affordable, and designed specifically for African farming conditions and practices.

3
The Solution

Gitako was built with deep understanding of local crops, farming cycles, market dynamics, and the unique challenges faced by African farmers.

4
The Impact

Today, Gitako serves over 1,000 farmers across 15 states, helping them increase yields by 25% on average and improve their livelihoods.
